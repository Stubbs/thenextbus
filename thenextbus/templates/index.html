This quick app is designed to display nicely on as many mobile devices as possible, mainly because the default HTML from TravelSouthYorkshire, or any of the YourNextBus services is so shocking, try The Next Bus on services in West Yorkshire, South Yorkshire, Belfast, Cardiff, Gloucestershire, Oxfordshire, Surrey and Kent

You can find the stop number either on the bus stop, or searching TSY's website. Once you find the stop number bookmark the URL (http://thenextbus.appspot.com/stop/<stop number>) on your phone for easy access next time
